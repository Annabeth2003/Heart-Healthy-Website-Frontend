Grilled Salmon with Avocado Salsa

Ingredients:

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Season the salmon fillets with salt and pepper.
  2. Grill the salmon over medium heat for 4-5 minutes per side.
  3. In a bowl, mix the diced avocado, red onion, lime juice, and olive oil.
  4. Top the grilled salmon with the avocado salsa and serve immediately.
