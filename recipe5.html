Fresh fruit kebabs

Ingredients:

  • 6 ounces low-fat, sugar-free lemon yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon fresh lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon lime zest
  • 4 pineapple chunks (about 1/2 inch each)
  • 4 strawberries
  • 1 kiwi, peeled and quartered
  • 1/2 banana, cut into 4 1/2-inch chunks
  • 4 red grapes
  • 4 wooden skewers

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the yogurt, lime juice and lime zest.
  2. Cover and refrigerate until needed.
  3. Thread 1 of each fruit onto the skewer.
  4. Repeat with the other skewers until the fruit is gone.
  5. Serve with the lemon lime dip.
