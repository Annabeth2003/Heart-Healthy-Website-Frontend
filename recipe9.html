Quinoa cakes

Ingredients:

  • 3 sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into spears
  • 1 cup uncooked quinoa
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 6 ounces Gruyere or Parmesan cheese, shredded
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat oven to 375 F. Place the potatoes on a greased baking sheet.
  2. Bake for 45 minutes or until potatoes are completely soft.
  3. Meanwhile, cook quinoa according to package directions; set aside to cool.
  4. In a large bowl, combine cooked potatoes, cooked quinoa, eggs, garlic, cheese, parsley, salt, pepper and nutmeg.
  5. Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large saucepan.
  6. Form half of the quinoa mixture into 1/4-cup patties and place in the pan; cook until cakes are golden brown.
  7. Place cooked patties on a baking sheet.
  8. Repeat process with remaining oil and quinoa mixture.
  9. Bake cakes in the oven for 5 minutes to ensure they are heated through.
